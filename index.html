Dillan's Amazing PVC Bows

PVC bows are amazing! They are very inexpensive to make, and you get a great value back from them. They are very durable and shoot very well. I love making my bows and enjoy selling and shooting them very much.

So how are they made? well they are first heated up with a heat gun, until they are soft enough to smash. Then they are smashed at an angle between a 2 by 4 and another board. Then it is heated again and formed by bending it around a pan. It is also formed with the hands by squishing and moving.
